Hi, I'm Miles!

I'm from Woodbury, MN (suburb of St. Paul). I recently graduated summa cum laude from Vanderbilt University with a double major in chemical engineering and applied math. I minored in computer science, physics, and chemistry. I currently work as a software engineer at BlackRock in Atlanta. I made the decision to pursue this career path while I worked in a chemical engineering research lab the summer after my sophomore year.

Independent projects I've worked on include this static personal webpage and an "Are You Smarter than a 5th Grader?" quiz game. The inspiration for this game came from YouTube video renditions of the original TV series. YouTubers go to college campuses and ask students relatively simple questions and it's really entertaining when the students get them wrong. You can find links to these pages in the nav bar above. This website was made with HTML and CSS, and is hosted on Github Pages. The quiz game was made using React and CSS.

I performed research in Dr. Jamey Young's lab in the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at Vanderbilt from January 2022 through September 2022. The lab focuses on metabolic engineering, and I focused on cyanobacteria in particular. I performed cyanobacterial genetic transformation, metabolite extraction and derivatization, GCMS, and GCMS data analysis. I also worked with another PhD student on a machine learning side project. Click the navbar link to read a paper I wrote summarizing my work.

In my freetime, I enjoy playing soccer, working out, and trying new restaurants in Atlanta. My other interests include chess, poker, cooking, interior design, smellmaxxing, and keeping up with the news/politics.
